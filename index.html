Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a wide range of technologies including JavaScript frameworks (React, Angular, Vue), backend technologies (Node.js, Python, PHP), mobile development (React Native, Flutter), and database systems (MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL).

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines after understanding your project requirements.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to keep your software up-to-date, secure, and performing optimally after launch.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials pricing models depending on project requirements. After discussing your needs, we'll recommend the most suitable approach.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We frequently integrate with existing systems and can work with your current technology stack or recommend improvements.

What industries do you specialize in?

While we've worked across various industries, we have particular expertise in healthcare, education, e-commerce, and business automation solutions.
